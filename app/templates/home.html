Dear user this is a small explanation on how to use my code. There will be two options, celsius and fahrenheit, You will choose one of these options and then enter a zip code of the desired location that you would like to know the current weather for. The results will show your local time, what the weather feels like and the name of the location.

Here are some example zipcodes that you are welcome to try;

New York - 10001

Beverly Hills - 90210

California - 90001

Colorado - 80001
